# Data Activator

## Purpose

Explain alerting patterns for logistics operations.

## Alert Examples

- Shipment delay exceeds 30 minutes.
- SLA breach risk is high.
- Vehicle engine temperature exceeds threshold.
- Warehouse temperature exceeds allowed range.
- No telemetry received for 10 minutes.
- Repeated delay on the same route.

## Design Rules

- Test the condition in KQL first.
- Avoid noisy alerts.
- Define an owner and escalation path.
- Include business meaning with every alert.
- Review alert effectiveness after demos and pilots.
